🧬 About Me

I am an academician, researcher, and institutional administrator dedicated to advancing the biological sciences and fostering regional entrepreneurial ecosystems. I serve a dual role at Maharaja Chhatrasal Bundelkhand University (MCBU), Chhatarpur, heading the Department of Biotechnology while concurrently teaching within the School of Studies in Zoology.


My foundational expert execution in protein biochemistry, molecular genetics, and biophysics was developed during my intensive doctoral training at the premier Department of Biotechnology, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Roorkee. My academic vision combines strict scientific evaluation in structural biology with the creation of modern incubation facilities to build bridges between educational findings and regional startup development.

🏢 Administrative & Innovation Leadership

Chhatrasal Incubation and Innovation Foundation (CIIF)

Serving as a core organizational driver establishing CIIF as a premier registered Section 8 company at MCBU. Actively structuring frameworks under the World Bank-supported RAMP Scheme and pioneering institutional MoUs with bodies like the Madhya Pradesh Laghu Udyog Nigam (MPLUN) to catalyze MSME innovations and micro-entrepreneurship.

Departmental Leadership

Overseeing comprehensive curriculum modernization, laboratory expansion, and admissions operations for the Department of Biotechnology. Orchestrated progressive, interdisciplinary updates to the M.Sc. Biotechnology policy to encourage enrollment from diverse branch science streams.

Curriculum & Statutory Boards

Actively engaged in higher education structural workflows across Madhya Pradesh, including mapping multi-disciplinary Swayam digital courses to state unified syllabi, coordinating central examinations, and refining modern semester system evaluations.

🔬 Research Competencies

My laboratory integrates experimental biophysics with molecular paradigms to dissect complex protein behavior under physiological and stress environments:

Biophysics & Protein Stability

Analyzing structural dynamics, folding kinetics, and conformational changes of chemokine orthologs and bacterial structural elements across varying pH gradients using sophisticated spectroscopy.

Advanced Nanobiotechnology

Synthesizing hollow silica nanospheres assisted by catanionic vesicle frameworks for targeted, sustained smart cargo and chemo-drug delivery profiles.

Genetics & Molecular Zoology

Investigating evolutionary origins of essential inflammatory motifs, molecular mechanisms of recombinant expressions, and genomic alterations.
